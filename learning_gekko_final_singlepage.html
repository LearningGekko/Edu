About Us

Learning Gekko is founded by CA Aayush Agarwal and Mr. Kiran Kumar Poddar (MBA), two passionate professionals committed to bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skills.

CA Aayush Agarwal brings strong expertise in finance, having worked with leading banks and in taxation consulting. His practical understanding of money, banking, and career realities allows students to get insights beyond textbooks.

Mr. Kiran Kumar Poddar, with an MBA and rich experience working with international and Indian brands, specializes in digital solutions, branding, and helping individuals secure valuable opportunities—including assistance in getting offers from financial institutions.

Our Mission

To empower students with the real-world skills they need to succeed—regardless of their academic marks. Whether you're a bright student held back by low confidence, or a highly skilled learner not fitting into the marks-driven system, we’re here to help you stand out and succeed in life, college, and career.

Our Story

We started Learning Gekko with one belief:

No student should miss out on their dream college or career just because they lack exposure to real-world knowledge.

From building confidence and communication skills to learning finance, interview prep, and digital tools—our goal is to give every deserving student the competitive edge they truly need.

Our Courses

At Learning Gekko, our courses are designed to build real-world skills that truly matter. Each program blends practical knowledge, live activities, and personal feedback, offered in both online and offline modes (with offline preferred for maximum impact).

  • Personality Development & Confidence Building: Master the art of self-presentation, first impressions, and personal grooming. Build lasting confidence through interactive activities, constructive feedback, and real-world practice.
  • Public Speaking & Body Language: Learn to speak with clarity, emotion, and impact. Overcome stage fear, master your posture, and take control of how you’re seen and heard.
  • Financial Literacy for Teens
    Taught by finance professionals, this module covers money management, budgeting, digital payments, banking, and basic investing—skills every teen must know.
  • Career Planning & Goal Setting
    Identify your strengths, explore modern career paths, and create a clear action plan for your future. Perfect for students unsure about “what next?”
  • Digital Skills for the Corporate World
    Learn essential tools like professional email writing, PowerPoint presentations, and MS Excel—skills that every job or college expects you to know from day one.
  • Resume Writing & Interview Preparation
    Get a professionally reviewed resume and sharpen your interview skills through mock sessions and real-time feedback.
  • And many more, customized by experts based on your unique requirements.
